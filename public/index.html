    Why a vote is worth $100
    $15,900,000,000 spent on the 2024 federal elections¹ ÷ 155,238,302 ballots cast = $102.43 per vote

    Certified at 1 vote = $100. A figure of speech — the Bureau rounds down.

    The rate

    ¹ Source: OpenSecrets, 2024 federal cycle. Regional rates exist too — the 2022 midterm discount (~$79), the Georgia-runoff premium (~$111) — but the Bureau rounds down.

    The allegation

    We don't allege corruption. We allege arithmetic. Every figure is a public FEC record of lawful contributions, rounded to the nearest $100,000. They disclosed; we divided.

    Do your homework

    This site never tells you whom to vote for. Who takes corporate money is your call — start with the Integrity Index and OpenSecrets. Neither is affiliated with this site. Yet.

    The catch

    A pledge here is a wish until you vote. Register in two minutes at vote.gov and make a plan. Voters with a plan actually show up. That part isn't a joke.
